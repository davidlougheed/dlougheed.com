David Lougheed GitHub

I am a Human Genetics M.Sc. student in Prof. Guillaume Bourque's lab at McGill University, studying short tandem repeats (STRs). I can be emailed at contact@dlougheed.com.

From May 2019 until August 2020, I worked as a software developer at the Canadian Centre for Computational Genomics (C3G) in Montréal.

I have a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Biology from McGill (graduated May 2019 with First Class Honours.)
